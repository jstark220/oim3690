About Me

Currently a senior at Babson College concentrating in Accounting. This summer, I will be interning in Audit at Deloitte in McLean, VA. My time at Babson has fueled my passion for both accounting and entrepreneurship, and this semester, I am working as an Intermediate Accounting II Tutor, where I hope to help students build confidence and technical skills in accounting. Before my time at Babson, I took a gap year after high school to create and solely manage StarkSensei Trading LLC, which generated $70,000+ in revenue with 50% profit margins through the sale of collectible trading cards. I continued my entrepreneurial passion when I got to Babson by co-founding Swipe Athletics, an innovative athletic apparel startup providing high-quality tennis shorts with an integrated towel so players can easily dry their hands.
