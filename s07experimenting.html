About Materiality Check

Materiality Check was founded to bridge the gap between complex corporate accounting and the investors, students, and professionals who need to understand it. We provide detailed, unbiased analysis of financial statements, earnings releases, and accounting policies from the world's leading companies.

Founded by Jacob Stark, an accounting student at Babson College and incoming audit intern at Deloitte, Materiality Check combines academic rigor with practical insights from real-world financial analysis. Our coverage spans major sectors including technology, financial services, healthcare, retail, energy, and communications.

We believe that informed decision-making starts with understanding the numbers. Through clear explanations and detailed breakdowns, we make accounting accessible without sacrificing depth or accuracy.
